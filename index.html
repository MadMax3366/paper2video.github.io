What is Paper2Video?

Paper2Video reads a research paper and produces a polished 60-second explainer with no human editing. It extracts the paper's main figure and result tables/plots, writes a content-rich 8-beat narration, lays out an animated HyperFrames composition with on-screen captions and figure call-outs, generates a voiceover, renders the video, and optionally composites a talking-head avatar narrator in the corner.

About

Paper2Video is an automated paper-to-video pipeline built on HyperFrames (write HTML, render video). Figures and tables are extracted from each PDF, an 8-beat narration is written and voiced, an animated composition is rendered, and an optional realistic talking-head avatar is composited in the corner.
